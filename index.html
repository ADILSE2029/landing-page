THE FOUR SEASONS

As the Earth orbits the sun, its tilted axis causes sunlight to hit different regions at different angles throughout the year. This creates four distinct seasons, spring, summer, autumn, and winter, each bringing its own changes in temperature, daylight, and the natural world around us
